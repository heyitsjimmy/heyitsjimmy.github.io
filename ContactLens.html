CONTACT LENS EVALUATION

A contact lens evaluation is performed on all patients that require a contact lens prescription. The evaluation is in addition to the comprehensive eye examination. It consists of an ocular health assessment, corneal curvature measurements, evaluation of the fit of the lenses on the eyes, and follow up visits to determine the vision and comfort of the lenses.
