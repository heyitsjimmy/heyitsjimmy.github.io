Dr. Anny Nguyen

Dr. Anny Nguyen is a therapeutic optometrist and optometric glaucoma specialist who is trained in the treatment and management of eye diseases. She earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Biology from the University of Texas at Dallas and a Doctorate of Optometry from the University of Houston College of Optometry. Her clinical rotations specialized in custom contact lens fitting, ocular disease, and pediatrics. Dr. Nguyen has been highly trained in the co-management of LASIK, ocular diseases such as glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, and age related macular degeneration. She also has a special interest in ocular allergy and dry eye management. She is a member of the Texas Optometric Association, American Optometric Association, and Dallas County Optometric Society.

Dr. Nguyen is committed to providing quality eye care and overall ocular health. She is a fun-loving and energetic doctor who will happily take care of you and your family’s eyes! Away from the office, Dr. Nguyen enjoys traveling, dancing, shopping, and spending time with her family and friends.
